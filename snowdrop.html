Snowdrop

Our Mission

Create an online store for users to buy and use quality gear with ease. Rest easy on the mountain knowing you have tried and true gear from people all around the world.

Problem

There are difficulties finding tried and true snowboard/skiing gear online. You have to travel to many different web apps to find certain brands you may be looking for that are trusted.

Solution

Creating the web app with all trusted brands to search through. Gear that has been tested and proven quality. You are able to read each of the customers reviews on the gear along with fit. Everything you need in one place.
